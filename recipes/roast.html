Mississippi Roast

Description

Mississippi Roast, also known as Slow Cooker Pepperoncini Pot Roast, is a flavorful and tender dish that has become a favorite among home cooks. This easy-to-make recipe involves slow-cooking a 4-pound beef chuck roast with just a few simple ingredients: butter, pepperoncini peppers, ranch dressing mix, and au jus mix. After cooking on low for 8 hours, the result is a melt-in-your-mouth roast infused with the tangy, savory flavors of the pepperoncini and seasonings. Perfect for serving over egg noodles or mashed potatoes, this dish is a comforting, crowd-pleasing meal that requires minimal effort for maximum flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) beef chuck roast
  • Â¼ cup butter
  • 5 pepperoncini peppers
  • 1 (1 ounce) packet ranch dressing mix
  • 1 (1 ounce) packet dry au jus mix

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place roast in a slow cooker. Form a pocket in the top of the roast and place butter, pepperoncini peppers, ranch dressing mix, and au jus mix in the pocket.
  3. Cook on Low for 8 hours.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
